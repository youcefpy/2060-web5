Our vision

We aspire to offer an open-standards-based network that will democratise the usage of verifiable credentials and SSI. Our goal is to empower end-users with control over data and privacy, while advancing trust between entities. trust between entities.

Similar to existing cloud services offered by prominent providers, this network will offer decentralised services essential for any Web5 application, such as storage, signalling, communication channels, and identity.

Our model operates on the belief that no intermediary is required for Web5. Therefore, anyone can participate as Operators by running the network and its services, while receiving rewards in doing so. Simultaneously, anyone can use the network and its services as Consumers by paying the required fees to the Operators.
