Our vision

We aspire to offer open-standards-based tools that will democratise the usage of Verifiable Credentials, and provide the bricks to build fully interoperable decentralised p2p and group communication chat and voice channels, with no vendor lock-in.

Our goal is to empower end-users with control over data and privacy, while advancing trust between entities.
